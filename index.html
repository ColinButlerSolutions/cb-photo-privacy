Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

CB Photo Capture allows authorized users to enter a subject identifier, capture white-light and blue-light photos and videos, and export capture packages for use in authorized study workflows.

Data handled by the app

The app may handle the following information on the device:

  • subject identifiers entered by the user
  • photos captured using the device camera
  • videos captured using the device camera
  • timestamps associated with captured media
  • exported ZIP packages created by the app

Data collection

CB Photo Capture does not use advertising, third-party tracking, behavioral analytics, data brokers, or advertising identifiers.

The app does not sell personal information.

The app is designed as a local capture and export utility. Captured files are stored locally on the device unless an authorized user exports or transfers them as part of the applicable Claris Bio workflow.

Clinical use

CB Photo Capture does not diagnose disease, analyze images, score clinical findings, recommend treatment, or make clinical decisions. The app only captures and exports photos and videos.

Data handling after export

Data exported from the app is handled according to applicable Claris Bio study procedures, privacy procedures, and data protection requirements.
